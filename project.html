    STUDY IN USA

    The USA is now the most popular country for international students and a top university destination choice for study abroad and hosts more international students than any other country in the world. Many international students choose U.S. colleges and universities for its academic excellence, variety of educational opportunities, cutting-edge technology, opportunity for research and training, flexibility, support services for international students, long-term career prospects and campus life experience.There will be many opportunities for you to gain practical, career-related experience during your US university studies. Cooperative (co-op) education and internship programs are widely available and often integrated into program curriculam to make it easy for you to build up your resume before graduation. On completion of your degree, you are legally authorized to work in the US in your related field for about a year.
